Trailsport

An outdoor and sporting goods company called Trailsport has conducted an email advertising campaign with the goal of increasing the sales of their loyalty card. By using customer surveys and online databases, they compiled a dataset with customer information. They used the dataset to send emails to known customers with a discount code for the loyalty card. They aim to take a data-driven approach to determine whether sending the emails was effective. They want to send more emails in the future, targeting customers who have a higher likelihood of signing up for the loyalty card. Additionally, they want to include product recommendations in these emails that match the customer's preferences. To that end, they have also formed a sales and products dataset.

Your challenge

You are a data engineer tasked with getting the data in the right format for a subsequent data analysis. The data scientist responsible for this has told you that he wants to use an XGBoost model and wants you to verify that the data will work when used for that model. The data that is available is static, but you are also tasked with thinking about how the data could flow if there were incremental data updates. For this he has asked you to make a visual representation of how you would process the data.

To summarize, you need to answer the following main questions:

  1. What steps need to be taken to clean the data such that it is usable in an XGBoost model?
  2. How would you handle incremental data updates?
  3. What would a visualization of the data flow look like?

Rules and tips

When preparing the business case please keep these rules in mind:

  • You can spend a maximum of 12 hours on this business case; this limit is set to simulate a working environment.
  • You are not allowed to share any of the documents or datasets received with other people or online.
  • The presentation should be about 15 minutes, after which a discussion of 15 minutes about the presentation will follow.
  • You are allowed to use the internet, but you are not allowed to ask other people for help.
  • We would prefer you write a solution in Python and/or SQL.
  • You need to send the presentation and your code to the specified contact person.

We also want to give you these tips:

  • Support your decisions with appropriate visual representations.
  • Think about what future steps for this project could look like.
  • Keep in mind that you are presenting your plan to a person who is not a data engineer.
